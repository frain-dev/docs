## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add your license key"> 
    <AddLicense />
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure your SAML Connection from the SSO Portal">
    <Frame caption="SSO Portal">
      <img src="/images/sso-portal.png" /> 
    </Frame>
    After you purchase a license with SSO, you'd get an email with a link to configure a `SAML Connection`. **Please ensure you only configure one `SAML Connection`**. Please reach out to us if you need to configure multiple connections.
  </Step> 

  <Step title="Ensure your Convoy instance can communicate with the SSO Server">
    To make sure sso works, make sure your instance can reach our servers at `ssoproxy.getconvoy.io`. The `SSO` server is responsible for generating `redirect uris` and validating `sso tokens`.
  </Step>
